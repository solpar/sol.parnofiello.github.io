This is my story

I hold a degree in Communication Sciences from the University of Buenos Aires. I wrote the first book on UX writing for the Spanish-speaking world: "UX Writing in Spanish. The ABC of the Discipline with Ñ". Today, I am a UX Manager with a focus on cognitive conversational interfaces. Since 2012, I have been working in user experience for major organizations in Latin America, especially in the financial sector (Mercado Libre, Ministry of Modernization of Argentina, ICBC Bank). I am the founder of the Spanish-speaking UX Writing community and Intuitiva UX, a consultancy specializing in user experience research and content.
